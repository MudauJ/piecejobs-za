## 1. WORKER EARNINGS DASHBOARD
Add a new "My Earnings" tab to the worker dashboard (/worker-dashboard).

This tab shows:
- Stats row at top:
  * Total Earned (all time) — sum of worker_payout from payments table where worker matches and status = 'released'
  * This Month — same filter but created_at within current month
  * This Week — same filter but created_at within current week
  * Pending — sum of worker_payout where status = 'held'

- Earnings chart: a simple bar chart showing earnings per month for the last 6 months (use recharts BarChart)

- Completed Jobs list: table showing each completed job with:
  * Job title
  * Homeowner suburb/city
  * Date completed
  * Amount earned (worker_payout)
  * Status badge (Paid/Pending)
